Features

Everything in one place

Manage your entire self-hosted media stack without switching between apps or browsers.

🎬

Movies

Browse your Radarr library, toggle monitoring, trigger auto or manual searches, and add new movies.

📺

TV Shows

View your Sonarr series, drill into seasons and episodes, toggle monitoring per season or episode, and trigger searches.

🎵

Music

Explore artists and albums via Lidarr, monitor releases, and search for missing albums from your music collection.

⬇️

Downloads

Monitor active Transmission torrents with live-updating details, per-torrent peers with optional country flags, and full tracker info.
